Why I wouldn't rush into this

There's one risk.

BuildProof currently has a very clear pitch.

Upload your code.

Get a security report.

People understand that in seconds.

If you suddenly change the homepage to:

Build anything with AI...

you've diluted that message.

Early-stage products benefit enormously from having one clear promise.
